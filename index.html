Flower Euphoria

Bringing Flowers to People One Day At a Time

Why Flower Euphoria?
Flowers brighten our days and fill us with joy and smiles. If we were sent flowers every day then our smiles would carry throughout the days and weeks to come. Flower Euphoria delivers a different flower everyday to your computer, tablet or phone to brighten your day and bring a smile to you. Not only will you see a new flower everyday but a little bit of information about that flower and any unique details that might pertain to it.
Functionality
Flower Euphoria provides a flower per day to the user. The application can sit on the desk top as a small window or minimized to the task bar. When the User wants a cherry disposition to come over them, they can maximize the application and view an image of a flower and some interesting details regarding that flower being displayed.
Upcoming Features
As Flower Euphoria grows and comes to evolve, the future concept of the Adobe AIR project will be to generate a user input for birthdays and other favorite events that they may schedule in a calendar. This will allow the user to view flowers for there respective birthday and any day that is of importance to them. Along with a custom calendar event, they will be able to link to a florist either in their immediate area or any online florist and order flowers for any occasion as well through the application.
